Navdy is a heads-up display with fleet management system

We address driving safety concerns, standardize business operations and minimize liability issues for companies by providing a heads up display to perform car activities (navigation, phone calling, etc.) for company drivers.

Time

Navdy allows drivers to conveniently record their time sheets either through an app or simply using their voices while driving.

Maps

Navdy supports Google Maps and pre-installed offline maps to navigate your driver to the destination reliably and quickly (even on areas without an internet).

Integration

Navdy is smart. Navdy integrates itself to drivers' vehicles to perform regular system check-ups. It will notify drivers and managers if there's an issue. It also checks vehicle's gas level to automatically reroute to nearest gas station if the vehicle is low on gas.

Benefits of Navdy

Keep your company drivers safe by having a Heads-up-Display to access different features on the phone without having them take their eyes off the road. This minimizes liabilities by reducing the number of accidents from driver distractions.

You can standardize the way your business operates by having a single platform to monitor communication and navigation.
